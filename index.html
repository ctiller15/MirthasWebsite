Mirtha's Latin Cafe

Mirtha's is what you get when you mix a Dominican and a Polk County native. We are a family run restaurant in the town of Dundee. We proudly serve our delicious meals to our community with our Dominican and Southern hospitality. Come enjoy some Dominican, Puerto Rican, and Cuban dishes. Leave some space for the coconut flan or bread pudding!
